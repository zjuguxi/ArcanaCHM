Why ArcanaCHM

Built for readers who care

Not another Electron wrapper. A native macOS app, engineered around four principles.

Blazing fast

A compiled SwiftUI binary launches in milliseconds. The WebKit rendering surface keeps scrolling buttery-smooth, even in 500 MB CHM archives.

Security-hardened

Path sandboxing isolates extracted CHM content from the rest of your system — malicious or malformed archives can't escape. Library state is auto-backed-up against corruption.

Intuitive by design

Built to macOS Human Interface Guidelines. Remembers your last page and scroll position, keyboard-first shortcuts, and a bilingual UI that follows your system language.

Completely offline

No telemetry, no accounts, no network calls — ever. Your documentation stays on your Mac, exactly where it belongs.
