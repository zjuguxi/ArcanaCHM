Why ArcanaCHM

Built for readers who care

A native macOS app built around four things readers actually care about.

Blazing fast

Opens instantly and scrolls smoothly, even with very large CHM files. No lag, no waiting.

Security-hardened

Opens files safely — anything inside a CHM can’t reach the rest of your Mac. Your library is backed up automatically, so nothing gets lost.

Intuitive by design

Follows macOS conventions so it feels familiar at once. Remembers where you left off, works with the keyboard, and speaks your language.

Completely offline

No accounts, no tracking, no internet needed. Your documents stay on your Mac, where they belong.
